Built for the rush

Everything the counter needs,
nothing it doesn't.

One elegant screen handles collection, delivery and every aggregator — then quietly takes care of the paperwork.

Kitchen tickets & VAT receipts

Tap items, pick the order type, take cash or card — the kitchen ticket and customer receipt print automatically, VAT calculated for you.

X & Z reports

Check the day's cash, card and VAT totals any time with an X report. Run a Z at close to bank the day, save a backup and reset for tomorrow.

Telegram alerts

Till opens, an order lands, a payment or refund happens — the manager gets a message instantly, naming the staff member, from anywhere.

Five order channels

Collection, Delivery, Deliveroo, Uber Eats and Just Eat — each tracked separately, so you always know where the money came from.

Safe daily records & cloud backup

Every day is written to JSON and CSV for the accountant — point Drive, Dropbox or OneDrive at the folder for an always-current cloud copy.

AI phone ordering · coming

An AI answers the shop phone, takes the order by voice and drops it straight onto the till and the kitchen. Designed in from day one.
