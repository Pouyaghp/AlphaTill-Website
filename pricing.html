Questions

Good to know

Is my data mine?

Completely. AlphaTill runs on your own hardware and writes daily records you keep — back them up to any cloud drive you like.

What hardware do I need?

A Windows computer and a receipt/kitchen printer. You can add a tablet on the same Wi-Fi to take orders around the shop.

When is AI phone ordering ready?

It's the top of our roadmap and designed into the system already. Join the AI Voice waitlist and we'll bring you on as it rolls out.
