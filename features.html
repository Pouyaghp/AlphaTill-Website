Refined for the counter.

Reporting

X any time. Z at close.

The X report gives the manager a live picture of the day. The Z report closes it cleanly — banking the totals, writing a backup, and resetting for tomorrow.

  • Live cash, card and VAT totals
  • Sales broken down by order type
  • Item-by-item — see exactly what sold
  • Z writes JSON + CSV backups and resets the day

Peace of mind

Know what's happening, even when you're not there.

AlphaTill sends the manager a Telegram message for everything that matters — and keeps a full activity log of every action behind the counter.
