About

Built for the rush.

AlphaTill started with a simple frustration: takeaway tills are either clunky and cheap, or polished but priced for chains. We wanted something that felt premium at the counter, stayed honest about your data, and gave the owner real visibility — even from home.

Fast where it counts

During a Friday-night rush, every second matters. The order screen is designed so staff barely think about it.

Your data, yours

Records live on your hardware and back up to a cloud drive you control. No platform holding your sales hostage.

Built for what's next

AI phone ordering, a menu editor and direct printing are on the way — AlphaTill is designed to grow with your shop.

The promise

A till that respects your time.

We measure success by the things you no longer have to think about: the receipt that printed itself, the totals that were already added up, the message that told you the till opened on time.

AlphaTill handles the paperwork so you can run the shop.
